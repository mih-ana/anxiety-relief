Developed to inspire you to live a healthier,
heartier, more buoyant life.

The goal of this application is to aid you in alleviating your anxiety by means of visual, aural, as well as kinesthetic stimulation. The main intentions are to support every person who is dealing with anxiety, to make it known that anxiety is not something to be ashamed about, as well as to encourage everyone to take a step forward in ameliorating their mental health.

Mental health forms an important part of a person's overall well-being, and for that reason this application is free, and acessible to everyone.
